About Us

Welcome to Huduu | هدوء

It is widely reported in the news media the effects the pandemic has had on relationships, mental health, and a host of other issues. Please know that Huduu is available to provide confidential assistance.

Huduu provides AUS students, employees, and dependents free confidential counseling services in support of positive mental health and well-being. Our male and female counselors can help with a variety of issues such as:

Anxiety

Depression

Relationship Concerns

Stress Management

Motivation

Self-esteem

Loss and Life Changes

Difficulty Managing Time

Family Issues

Other Issues

Expert Counselors

Licensed counselors who specialize in helping clients develop better cognitive and emotional skills and reduce symptoms of mental illness to improve their lives.

Booking System

Choose from a variety of Huduu's counselors and book a suitable session for yourself from the comfort of your home by filling a simple form.

Track Your Progress

Huduu keeps track of your progress each day that you login and use it, allowing you to note down your progress and growth over a period of time.

Tips from Counselors

Receive general or personalized tips from Huduu's counselors frequently and stay up to date with their beneficial recommendations.
